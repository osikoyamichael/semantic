Aristotle:
He was born in the city of Stagrira in northen greece during the classical period.his father, Nicomachus,died when Aristotle was a child and he was brought up by a guardain.At 17 or 18 he joined Plato's Academy in Athens and remained there till the age of 37, shortly after Plato died, Aristotle left Arthens and, at the request of Philip II of Macedon, tutored his son Alexander the Great beginning in 343 BC.He established a library in the Lyceum which helped him to produce many of his hundred of books on papyrus scrolls

Life:
In general, the details of Aristotle's life are not well-establised.The biographies written in ancient times are often speculative and historians only agree on a few salient points. More than 2300 years after his death,Aristotle remains one of the most influential people who ever lived.He contributed to almost every field of human knowledge then in existences and he was the founder of many few field.
